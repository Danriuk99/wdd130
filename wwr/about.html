White Water Rafting

We didn't start this company to stay inside the lines. We started it because we believe life is best lived at full throttle, with a paddle in hand and a wave crashing over the bow. Founded by a tight-knit crew of lifelong river guides, White Water Rafting was born out of a pure obsession with the rush of the river.

History

What started in 1999 with just a couple of secondhand rafts and a relentless passion for the water has grown into susquehanna's premier river outfitter
